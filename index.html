✦ Tour Details

Our Carefully Curated
Itinerary & Treks

Day 1: Arrival & Guwahati

Begin your adventure with a warm welcome at Guwahati. Enjoy local Assamese cuisine and a sunset cruise on the mighty Brahmaputra river before resting for the trek ahead.

Day 2: The Cherrapunji Drive

A scenic drive into the clouds. We'll stop at majestic viewpoints, navigate misty mountain passes, and settle into our premium eco-camp near the Cherrapunji valleys.

Day 3: Living Root Bridges

Embark on a guided hike down to the legendary double-decker living root bridges of Nongriat. Experience the bio-engineering marvels built by the indigenous Khasi tribes.

Day 4: Canyons & Waterfalls

Witness the awe-inspiring Nohkalikai Falls and explore the sprawling Laitlum Canyons. We conclude with a cultural bonfire evening before your departure.
